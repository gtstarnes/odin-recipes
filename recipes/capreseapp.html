Caprese Appetizer

Description

Colorful caprese salad skewers with bite-sized portions.

Ingredients

  • 20 Grape Tomatoes
  • 10 ounces mozzarella cheese, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 20 toothpicks

Steps

  1. Toss tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, olive oil, basil, salt, and pepper together in a bowl until well coated.
  2. Skewer one tomato and one piece of mozzarella cheese on each toothpick.
